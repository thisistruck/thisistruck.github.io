5 POUNDS FROM EACH TICKET SOLD TO CHARITY!

Posted on 13/07/2011 in News - Tags: charity donations, featured, tickets, Truck 14

Three of our favourite Oxfordshire-based charities are Sumatran Orangutan Society, Earth Trust (formerly the Northmoor Trust) and RSPB. They all make a real difference, preserving wildlife and habitats in Britain and abroad, and form this year’s Concert4Conservation fund.

Truck Festival are supporting these causes by donating £5 of every Truck Festival ticket purchased online today (13th July) to this fund, so if you haven’t already got your Truck ticket, head over to our ticket page and buy yours now, supporting these 3 fantastic charities in the process!
