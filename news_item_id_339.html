Fun Things To Do This Weekend..
Date submitted: Friday 8th August 2008

TODAY (Friday)- If you're prowling around the Cowley Road in Oxford today there's a fun sounding gig on at The Bullingdon Arms- Jason Ringenberg (ex Jason and the Scorchers), with Piney Gir and Emit Bloch. Tickets are £7, - why not take the family to Jason's kids show too? at 2pm  his alter ego Farmer Jason as well as Nick Cope from the Candyskins play at Fusion Arts (behind East Ox Community Centre). Profits from both shows go to Cancer Research.

also, DANNY & THE CHAMPIONS OF THE WORLD are at fine fancy-dress fandango TAPESTRY GOES WEST.

TOMORROW (Saturday) -TRUCK AFTERNOON at ROTA, Notting Hill Arts Club- FREE ENTRY!

What better way to while away the hours of a Saturday afternoon in London than in the bohemian surrounds of Notting Hill rts Club? The event runs from 4 til 8 and is FREE to get in!
Alongside Truck DJs, this wacky crowd are appearing:
Tristan & The Troubadours, The Winchell Riots & Alan MX

meanwhile, DANNY & THE CHAMPIONS OF THE WORLD are at SUMMER SUNDAE

and on Sunday, The Champs are at BLOOM FESTIVAL.
