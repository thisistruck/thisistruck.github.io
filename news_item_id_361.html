TRUCK ARTS WORKSHOPS!
Date submitted: Tuesday 23rd December 2008

Firstly, we'd like to wish all Truckers a very merry Christmas and a happy New Year! We like to keep busy in these winter months, so we'd like to tell you about something we are doing that will be of interest to 8-14 year olds in the Oxford area (and their parents). If you're not in this category yourself, please do spread the word. Following fantastic workshops at Hinksey Heights and Abingdon Children's Centre, Truck is putting on ten weeks of youth art workshops at Fusion Arts in East Oxford. Come along to these creative workshops in which all sorts of artistic explorations will take place! Children will be encouraged to explore their own ideas and imaginations with access to a wide range of materials. Projects will include drawing, printmaking, painting, sculpture, textiles and more...

These are for 8-14 year olds and will take place every Monday from 4-6pm.

Start date: Monday 12th Jan 1009, running time: 10 weeks

Location: Fusion Arts, East Oxford Community Centre
Princes Street, Oxford

£50 for 10 weeks inc. all materials or £7 for single session

To register call 01235 821 262, email info@thisistruck.com or check out the WORKSHOPS section of our website where you can also find out about workshops we are running at Cornerstone Arts in Didcot.
