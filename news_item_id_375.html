Bands: Last chance to submit your music!
Date submitted: Tuesday 31st March 2009


 

TOMORROW, April 1, 2009 is the final DEADLINE to submit applications to play at this year's Truck festival via Sonicbids.

Truck Twelve takes place on July 25th / 26th 2009 at Hill Farm, Steventon.

We have already confirmed 2 legendary headliners that we are VERY excited about and we think you will be too.

We are making TWELVE slots available at the festival for bands who have submitted through Sonicbids, this is 11 more slots than we have allocated to demo submissions in the past, so for your chance to join this year's line-up get your submission in BEFORE THE END OF THE DAY TOMORROW.

This is the only way we are accepting submissions for this year's event, so please DON'T send us a myspace link or a CD. Thanks to all those who have read the info on our website and submitted already, the standard has been impressive. If you are selected we will contact you before June 1st to ask you to play.


HOW DO I SUBMIT?
Just click on this LINK and upload whatever you'd like us to see in your Electronic Press Kit (EPK)-  music, photos, etc.
