Greetings from New York!
Date submitted: 12 Apr 2010 10:48 PM

TRUCK HQ has moved to the USA for the rest of the month as we prepare for the first ever TRUCK AMERICA. Robin & Joe have already played warmup shows with the DREAMING SPIRES and COMMON PRAYER in New York and Philadelphia. Our friend Will cycled to meet us at Heathrow Airport with freshly-printed scores for the Mercury Rev orchestra, which will be forming at the festival, and we're looking forward to rehearsing some of our favourite songs with the Rev. Tickets are available from the ticket page.
