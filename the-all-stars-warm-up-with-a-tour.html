The All-stars warm up with a tour!

Posted on 01/07/2011 in News - Tags: featured, The Dreaming Spires, Truck

TRUCK ALL-STARS WARM-UP SHOWS

By day we’re busy getting Hill Farm ready for Truck Festival, by night from July 4th we’ll be playing a series of festival warm-up shows with our band The Dreaming Spires. Taking two other Truck Festival acts out on the road with us, Two Fingers of Firewater and James Walbourne, we’ll be bringing a little taste of the festival to the following venues, kicking off with an Independence Day celebration of Americana at The Lexington in London:

Mon 4th July The Lexington,London

Tue 5th July South Street Arts Centre, Reading

Thu 7th July Prince Albert, Brighton

Fri 8th July The Boileroom, Guildford

Sun 10th July Guava Bar, Southampton

This might all seem like Second-hand news, but Robin, Joe & Loz will be Going their own way, perhaps road-testing  some classic rock covers as well. Could this Chain of shows be the first steps of the Rumoured all-stars cover show on the sunday night of the festival?
