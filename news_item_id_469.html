TRUCK America Schedule
Date submitted: 20 Apr 2010 11:32 PM

We've just posted the final(ish) timings for TRUCK America to the Stages & Line-ups page- if you're attending, you can start planning your itinerary. We were just at the Full Moon Resort last weekend for a wedding and we can tell you that it's no more than a 2-minute jog between the stages. As you can see, we've made sure they don't clash so you can see EVERY band (if you are crazy enough). You may notice we have added the fantastic Joan as Policewoman to the Sunday bill!

See our TICKETS page for info on Weekend & Day tickets.

For those that won't be joining us, there should be a wealth of video and audio coverage after the event, and even reports on BBC Oxford. Keep your eyes peeled next week for further line-up announcements for TRUCK UK and WOOD...
