CHAMPS single of the week on Radio 2!
Date submitted: 13th March 2008

Thankyou everyone who voted for 'The Truest Kind' by Danny and the Champions of the World- it won Radcliffe & Maconie's Single of the Week! If you're in London come and celebrate at The Lock Tavern (Thursday 13th March) where Danny plays solo or at The Windmill (Friday 14th March) where it's the full fandango! Keep up to date with Danny's mystic blog posts and become a Champion here.

Also appearing at The Windmill are Holton's Opulent Oog, Emma Tricca and Ain, along with all the usual face painting, lights and pictures, the cosmic yeehaa, and of course, Yesness!! If you don't know what these are, head down Blenheim Gardens, Brixton Hill to find out. More info here.
