HARVEST! Saturday 13th & Sunday 14th September 2008
Date submitted: Tuesday 19th August 2008

The makers of WOOD and TRUCK festivals join forces with Jonathan and Jane, owners of the Isis Farmhouse in Oxford, to celebrate the Harvest Moon! Click HERE for tickets.
Two stages of music overlooking the meadow:
Saturday 13th: Hot Club of Cowtown , Coute Diomboulu feat. Jali Fily Cissokho, Piney Gir & the Age of Reason, Gabriel Minnikin
Sunday 14th: The Magic Numbers, Danny & the Champions of the World, Coley Park, President Sweetheart + a very special Western Swing workshop with Hot Club of Cowtown

more acts to be announced on both days.
Doors 11am, Music starts at 1pm
Talks, Workshops, Crafts, Games and Nature walks
Like those dimly-remembered Harvest festivals of our youth, only better!

No muddy festival camping - sleep in your own bed!
Easy access on foot to Oxford centre, or why not come by bike or boat?
The Isis is situated on the Thames towpath between Donnington Bridge and Iffley Lock

Capacity strictly limited for this special event-
Tickets £20 per day, Combined Harvester weekend pass- £30. Under 14s free. Click HERE or head to :
The Isis, Videosyncratic (Cowley Rd/Summertown), Mostly Books (Abingdon), Toby English Books (Wallingford)

The Isis Farmhouse serve food and drink, using organic and local suppliers where possible-
Please do not bring your own. Cotswold Lager and Wheat Beer on tap!

See you there..
