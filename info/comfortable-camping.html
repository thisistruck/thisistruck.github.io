CAMPING

Your standard Truck weekend ticket includes 3 nights camping. This year, for the first time, Truck have a dedicated Family Field for you and your little Truckers.

A Campervan Pass is also available in advance, priced at £40.

Comfortable Camping

Don’t really see yourself as the camping sort? Take the hassle out of pitching your own tent with our range of pitch-perfect camping alternatives for you, your family and friends to enjoy.

Leave Your Tent at Home

Imagine not lugging tents and camping gear around for hours until you find a spot to pitch. No struggling to put up a tent in the dark. No calling for your mates across a pitch black campsite. Tangerine Fields is what you’re dreaming of. And it’s here at Truck Festival 2011.

Tangerine Fields have ready-pitched and fully equipped tents for hire. All you have to do is book and turn up. Tents are available in 2, 4, 6 and 8 person sizes. Better still, book with your friends and we’ll make sure you’re all camped together.

For more information and bookings, visit the Tangerine Fields Truck Festival page

Tipis and Yurts

Live out your Nomadic dreams in one of our splendid and spacious tipis. With room enough to swing not only your cat, but all your festival-going gear too, you’ll wonder why you even considered inserting pole A through webbing loops C and D!

Alternatively, bed down in one of our many yurts. Yurts have been used for thousands of years, after they originated in Central Asia, but not to worry, ours are pretty new. Snuggle up in the cosy cushions and warming, wooly sheepskins and you’ll completely forget the other times when you made do with a ground sheet and yesterday’s clothes.

Unfurnished 16ft Tipi (Sleeps 2-6) £250 - includes groundsheet and rugs.

Furnished 16ft Tipi (Sleeps 2-6) £320 - includes groundsheet, rugs, low tables, bed, bedding, cushions and paraffin lamp.

Unfurnished 10ft Yurt (Sleeps 2) £220 - includes groundsheet, door mat, carpet and shelving.

Furnished 10ft Yurt (Sleeps 2) £280 - includes mattress, clean linen*, sheepskins, cushions, table, lantern.

Unfurnished 12ft Yurt (Sleeps 4-6) £320 - includes ground sheet, door mat, carpet and shelving.

Furnished 12ft Yurt (Sleeps 4-6) £380 - includes mattress, clean linen*, sheepskins, cushions, table, lantern.

*Please note that Clean Linen includes pillows, but you’ll still need to bring your duvets or sleeping bags - it could get awful chilly without them!

For More information and bookings, please visit the Yurt Events Truck Festival page.
