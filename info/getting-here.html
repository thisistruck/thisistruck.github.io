Cycle to Truck

This year’s cycle to Truck convoy will begin at Oxford Cycle Workshop Training centre on Glanville Road, Oxford on Friday 22nd July at 4pm. Be there for 3.30pm to be ready for the 4pm departure.

The route will take about an hour and ambles through beautiful Oxfordshire back roads on our way to the festival site in Steventon. The ride will be at a casual pace and will be accompanied by a trained bicycle mechanic to solve any problems that might arise. The distance is a little over 10 miles, which even ridden very leisurely will get us there before 5.30pm.

You’ll be able to leave your luggage in safe hands at the start of the ride, and we’ll transport it for collection on your arrival. Anyone arriving by bicycle will be rewarded with a free beer token!

How you return from the festival is up to you, but we’ll be accompanying a ride back from the festival site at 10am on Monday morning. The luggage of cyclists will be collected and delivered to Oxford Cycle Workshop Training on Glanville Road for collection on Monday and Tuesday from 12noon until 9pm. Any further enquiries email dan (AT) ocwt.coop

CAR-SHARE

If you would like to reduce your carbon footprint, help other people travel to Truck Festival and save money, why not offer your empty car seats to others on goCarShare?

goCarShare is built around Facebook; you can login quickly and look at people’s profiles before deciding whether you would like to travel with them.

goCarShare is a green, fun and good value way to get to Truck Festival. Just add a car share request with Steventon as the destinationhere.

CAR PARKING

Parking Pass £8 in advance or £15 on the gate. Please remember to place your Truck sticker in your windscreen before you arrive at the festival. It saves time on arrival and looks nice too.
