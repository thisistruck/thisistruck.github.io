Tickets

Tickets for the all-new 3-day TRUCK FESTIVAL (at Hill Farm Steventon, July 22nd/23rd/24th 2011) are now available online and in person from TRUCK STORE and other local shops.

Weekend tickets include access to the festival from Midday Friday 22nd July, and include 3 nights camping (Friday, Saturday & Sunday). Prices from Monday are as follows:

Adult Weekend (includes 3 nights camping) - £99

Young Person (13-17) Weekend - £79. Under 18s must be accompanied by a responsible adult. See below for full conditions.

Friday day ticket - (includes Bellowhead, The Duke & The King, and the Transgressive Records day with Graham Coxon, Johnny Flynn and more) - £40 Adult / £33 Young Person (13-17)

Saturday day ticket ( includes Gruff Rhys, Roddy Woomble, and the Heavenly Recordings day with Saint Etienne, Edwyn Collins and more) - £45 adult, £38 Young Person (13-17)

Sunday day ticket (includes Truck Festival All-Stars, The Go! Team, Tunng, and the Bella Union day with John Grant, Philip Selway and more) - £40 Adult / £33 Young Person (13-17)

Minor (0-12) Weekend - Free

Car Parking - £8 adv / £15 on the day

Camper Vans - £40

Age Restrictions (note change of policy):

Truck Festival is committed to ensuring everyone who comes to our event has a safe and enjoyable time. Following consultation with Thames Valley Police, we have reviewed our under-18 policy as follows: Those aged 13 - 17 (with Young Person tickets) must be accompanied by an over-18 (adult) who accepts responsibility for them. One adult cannot take responsibility for more than 4 young persons. This adult must accompany the young persons on entry to the festival.

Should a 13 - 17 year old be deemed to be acting irresponsibly or against the conditions of entry then they, and the accompanying adult, will risk being evicted from the festival site.

This ‘adult’ can be an accompanying friend, or a parent or guardian.

Minors (Children aged 12 and under) will only be admitted accompanied by a parent or guardian and do not count, within reason, towards the allowance of 4 under-18s per adult. Contact details will be taken at the Gate for parents during the festival.

For more information, or if you have special particular circumstances please do not hesitate to contact the festival office by email to info (at) thisistruck.com.
