Tickets for Truck Thirteen are on sale now!

To celebrate TRUCK becoming a teenager we have actually REDUCED the price for actual human teenagers; a TEEN TRUCKER ticket for those aged 13-17 is now £60. TRUCK is still free for those 12 and under (14-and-unders must be accompanied by a parent or guardian) so it's also great value for families. We are also introducing a £15 Friday ticket for those who want to camp a night early - there will be some bands and DJs as well as food and drink on the Friday evening. To secure yourself a ticket now to enjoy both TRUCK and WOOD festivals, get yourself a superTRUCKER ticket here.
