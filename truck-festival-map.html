Transport

How you getting there? Save yourself some money - and the enviroment too by travelling on one of our special TRUCK buses. It's not a TRUCK it's just a normal bus, with comfy seats and plenty of room for your bags and belongings. We might even see if the driver will put the radio on...You'll get dropped off right at the festival site - and picked up again when all is said and done.

The Buses leave from The Cowley Road, for all you Oxfordites, and from Didcot Train Station for all of you from a little further afield. Didcot station is on the Great Western line and you can get there direct from Bristol, London, South Wales and even The North.
