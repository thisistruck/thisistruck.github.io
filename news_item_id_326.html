Magnificent Revolution bring the all night bicycle-powered party to Truck 11!
Date submitted: Thursday July 10th

We're very excited to announce that after their smash-hit debut at WOOD festival at Braziers Park in May the lovely folks from the Magnificent Revolution will be bringing their bicycle powered fun to Truck. Get down there and pedal yourself some power while grooving to a fabulous mixture of sounds from Reggae through to Drum n Bass via some party classics and everything else in between.. PLUS new movies from Hog's selection of up and coming independent fim-makers. You'll find them down the far end of the Market field, in the area previously manned by the disco shed (who have sadly left us for greener pastures -  we wish them all the best). Watch out for some other strange and wonderful treats down that end of the site too, more information to follow soon..

To learn more about the Magnificent Revolution go to www.magnificentrevolution.org
