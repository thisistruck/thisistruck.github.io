Stages & Lineups

Thanks to everybody that came - the weather was beautiful, the music was great, and it all made for a special weekend! NYC Taper has a couple of full sets from Mercury Rev and The Joy Formidable, as well as a full overview of the weekend, Brooklyn Vegan have a full photo-diary of the festival, and CLASH magazine have a review of the festival. Look out for videos from the weekend, as well as checking out the Truck America twitter for other links and goodies!

Until next time, Truck on!
