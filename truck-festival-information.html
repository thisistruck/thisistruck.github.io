Information
The 13th TRUCK FESTIVAL takes place at Hill Farm, Steventon July 23rd -25th July 2010. For the first time if you want to start the party early you can buy yourself a Friday night ticket.

Arrival Times
Car Park and Main Gates open at 9am on Saturday, and 9am on Sunday. For Friday night ticketholders the Car Park opens at 5pm on Friday.                 

There is now CAMPING on FRIDAY, July 23rd, 2009 - there will be entertainment, food and drink available so start the party early and get yourself a Friday ticket as well.
 

 

Music Times 

Saturday 12 midday - 2.30 am ,  Sunday 10am -10pm   

Food and Drink  

There will be a great selection of food, from bacon sandwiches to curries and of course TRUCK's famous hangover cure, Banana Smoothies. All the food is cooked by The Rotary Club and all the profits go to charity. The reasonably priced bar (£3 a pint) will be stocked with great local beers and ciders, and “manned” by our cross dressing bar-staff.


Age Restrictions  Children aged 12 and under are free. Ages 14 and under must be accompanied by a paying parent or guardian; contact details will be taken at the Gate for parents during the festival.
 
Trucking
We would encourage you to try to reach Truck using public transport – the traffic jams on the way in and out of the village can get pretty bad! The easiest way to arrive is by our dedicated TRUCK BUS.  There will be TRUCK BUSES running from Oxford (Cowley Road) costing £3 each way.  There will also be a bus running from Didcot Train Station for £2 each way.
 
Camping
Your standard Truck weekend ticket includes 2 nights camping (Saturday & Sunday).  There is a separate Quiet camping / Family camping area. There is a separate Campervan area.

Campervans require a campervan pass , priced £20 advance or £30 on the gate, available from all online ticket sellers.

Not really a camper? Well why not stay in one of our beautiful yurts.

We have beautiful and cosy yurts available for hire.
All of them come ready to move into with woven bed matts, bed linen, a mirror to check your hair in the morning and other treats to make your stay even more comfortable.

Small yurts (sleeps 2-3) and larger yurts which sleep about 6people.

For more information and to book your yurt please get in touch with Fred. www.yurtevents.co.uk
email - Fred@yurtevents.co.uk  Tel - 07974139078


 
Car Parking
Tickets £8 adv or £15 on the gate. Please remember to place your Truck sticker in your windscreen BEFORE you arrive at the festival, this will save everyone time on arrival.  

Tickets
We are sad to say that we wont be using WeGotTickets this year – instead we will be working with our friends at Gigantic, as well as supporting a brand new ticketing agency called Brown Paper Tickets – who are the first “fair trade” ticketing agency in the UK, operating in a Not-just-for-profit fashion.   There's a whole new set of tickets this year so see our TICKETS page for more info. Including a brand new Friday night ticket – get the party started early!  * Don't worry about remembering all of this as there will also be an information point in the Market field for the duration of the Festival*
