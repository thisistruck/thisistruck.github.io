Robin

Born in the Seventies, Robin is/was a member of Whispering Bob, Goldrush, and Dusty Sound System, and has produced material by all 3.


Rowland

Rowland has been part of the Truck Studio since it opened for business in late 1999, and has overseen projects for many happy customers since the tape (or digital simulation thereof) began to roll. His recording career began when he was but a short-wearing youngster and has taken him from humble beginnings to the heights of working with Bananarama, and making cups of tea for Led Zeppelin's Robert Plant. Rowland's tea is unsurpassed in quality.
