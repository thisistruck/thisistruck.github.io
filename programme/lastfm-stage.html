Last.fm Stage

Due to the success of last year, Last FM will be returning with a stage of their own. On Friday BBC Oxford Introducing will be this year’s host and curator, ensuring a line-up that includes the finest cutting edge music the county has to offer. On Saturday Oxford’s vibrant and most talked about ‘musical movement’ Blessing Force will be taking charge, supplying a truly diverse and exciting line-up of Oxford artists. If that doesn’t quench your musical thirst, Sunday will see a line-up curated by our very own Truck Store!
