We've got six stages at Truck, offering a musical cornucopia of everything from Indie-Rock to Americana, Country to Post-Rock, and from earnest singer-songwriters to the bleeps, beeps and dirty bass at the Cowshed rave.

Music Times
Friday 6pm - Midnight
Saturday 12 midday - 2.30 am
Sunday 10am -10pm

THE TRUCK STAGE


The original main stage at TRUCK – still using the same flat bed trailer we used in 1999, the reason behind the name but bigger and better than ever before.

THE BARN

 

Yeah, it holds cows the rest of the year, but during the festival it becomes THE BARN STAGE, during the day it plays host to the buzziest new bands and on saturday night it transforms with lazer powered intensity for the Cow-shed Rave.

 


THE MARKET STAGE

Playing host to the more acoustic and country based offerings at TRUCK, Sunday will be hosted by radio legend and Steventon resident 'Whispering' Bob Harris

THE BEAT-HIVE


A 20m diameter geo-desic dome catering for those that like a higher BPM than the average TRUCKER, bleep, beeps, bangs and dancing. Last year saw Errors and Daedulus positively smash it.

 

last.fm presents... THE VILLAGE PUB


Based next to the TRUCK bar for easy access, the Village pub is the place to discover the latest talent. This year it's sponsored by last.fm, which we love 'cos it's another great way to discover music.


THE PAVILION

This stage returns hosting all kinds of performance poetry, comedy, cabaret and solo acoustic performances, in partnership with comedy from THE FREE BEER SHOW, acoustic open-mic from the long-running CATWEAZLE CLUB, spoken-word poetry slams hosted by HAMMER & TONGUE, and royal ribaldry in spades from QUEEN OF CLUBS.

 

 

...And in the far field throughout the weekend and late into the night...

 

 

BOXFORD TUNE DIVERSITY & LION ENTERTAINMENT

A new addition to TRUCK this incandescent stage provides a peripheral party late into the dark of the night, with Fresh Out The Box party DJ sets at peak times, and laid back electronica and deep sub dub for when your head and feet need a rest.

KIDS TENT MUSIC

Keep the little 'uns entertained with sing-alongs, and musical munchkins.
