TRUCK won the award for Best Festival at the 2008 Indy Awards last night! We'll put up some pictures when we can work out how to get them off the phone. Modern technology. If you're in the vicinity of Reading, do come along to our official WOOD launch party at the Oakford Social Club (right next to the station, and entry is FREE!) from 5 -11pm on Sunday featuring... Danny & The Champions of The World, Holton's Opulent Oog, The Elks, Band of Hope and Coley Park DJs.
